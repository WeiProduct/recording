Privacy Policy

AI Voice Notes is a WeiProduct app for recording, importing, transcribing, and organizing audio-based notes.

Local Data

Recordings, transcripts, and notes should be treated as user-owned content. The app experience is designed around local capture and explicit platform permissions.

Permissions

The product may request microphone access for recording, speech recognition access for transcription, file access for imports, and notifications for completion alerts when enabled by the user.

AI Transcription

When a user chooses an AI transcription path, audio may be processed by the selected transcription service. Privacy-sensitive recordings can use local Apple speech recognition where available.

Website

This website does not require account creation. If you contact WeiProduct by email, your email address and message are used to respond to your request.
